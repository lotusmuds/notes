Rava Dosa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rava / semolina / suji, coarse
  • 1/2 cup rice flour, fine
  • 1/4 cup maida / all purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon yogurt/curd (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 cup water
  • 1 chilli, finely chopped
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon jeera, cumin
  • few curry leaves, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoon coriander, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • oil for roasting

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, put 1/2 cup rava, 1/2 cup rice flour, 1/4 cup and maida, 1 tbsp curd, 1 tsp salt and 2½ cup water.
  2. Whisk and mix well making sure there are no lumps.
  3. Add 1 chilli, 1 inch ginger, ½ tsp pepper, 1 tsp cumin, few curry leaves, 2 tbsp coriander and 1 onion.
  4. Add 1½ cup water and prepare a watery consistency batter.
  5. Rest the batter for 20 minutes making sure the rava has absorbed water.
  6. After 20 mins mix well making sure the batter is thin watery consistency.
  7. Now carefully pour the dosa batter over very hot tawa.
  8. Lower the flame and cook until the dosa turns golden and crisp.
  9. Serve.
Tips:
  • Be sure to add water according to recipe to get the batter coonsistency appropriate
