Fluffy Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat the egg with milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and gently stir until combined. Do not overmix.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with syrup, fresh fruits, or your favorite toppings.
Tips:
  • Do not overmix the batter, it keeps the pancakes fluffy.
  • Use medium heat to prevent burning.
  • You can add chocolate chips or blueberries for extra flavor.
