Dosa batter Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 vati brown rice
  • 1 vati udit daal
  • 1/3 vati pohe
  • 1 table spoon methi dana
  • Approximately 2 table spoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon hinga

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, put 3 vati brown rice, 1 vati udit daal, 1/3 vati pohe, 1 tbsp methi dana.
  2. Mix ingredients in the bowl and wash twice with water.
  3. Soak in water twice the height of the washed ingredients and let it sit for 8 to 10 hours.
  4. Drain the water after 8 to 10 hours.
  5. Grind the soaked ingredients (may need 1 vati for grinding per mix).
  6. Adjust consistency keep for fermentation with a cover on top in warm area.
  7. It will take 12 hrs in summer and atleast 24 hours in winter.
  8. After fermentation adjust consistency.
  9. Put salt 2 tablespoons.
  10. Put hinga 1/4 teaspoon.
  11. You can make dosa or idli from this batter.
  12. Serve.
Tips:
  • Be sure to adjust water to get the batter coonsistency appropriate
