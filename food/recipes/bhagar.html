Varicha Bhat/Bhagar/Soma seeds Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 vati Soma Seeds/Soma seeds/Vari.
  • 1 and 1/2 vati Water.
  • 1 teaspoon Jeera.
  • 1/2 Tomato.
  • 1 tablespoon Ghee melted.
  • 3 slited Mirchi.
  • 1 teaspoon Salt.
  • 1 teaspoon Sugar.
  • 1 tablespoon Peanut powder.

Instructions

  1. Heat cooker pan low flame.
  2. Add ghee.
  3. After ghee is heated put jeera.
  4. After jeera is tad tad put mirchi.
  5. After mirchi is tad tad put tomato.
  6. Let it saut√© put 1 teaspoon salt and 1 teaspoon sugar.
  7. Put samo seeds and stir and let it roast.
  8. Put water stir everything and put 1 tablespoon peanut powder.
  9. Put in cooker 8 shitti or keep stiring the pan for 10 to 15 mins till Vari is cooked.
  10. Serve.
Tips:
  • You can also make it without using pressure cooker on just the pan.
